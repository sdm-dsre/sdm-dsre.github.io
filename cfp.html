Important dates

  • Submissions Due - February 15, 2020 (11: 59 P.M. AoE)
  • Notification - March 15, 2020
  • SDM pre-registration deadline: - April 2, 2020
  • Camera ready: - April 15, 2020
  • SDM DSRE Full day Workshop - May 9, 2020

We invite quality research contributions, position and opinion papers addressing relevant challenges in the domain of Retail & E-Commerce. We invite submission of papers and posters of two to ten pages (including references), representing original research, preliminary research results, proposals for new work, position and opinion papers. All submitted papers and posters will be single-blind and will be peer reviewed by an international program committee of researchers of high repute. Accepted submissions will be presented at the workshop.
